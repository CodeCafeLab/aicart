# **App Name**: StudioForge AI

## Core Features:

- AI Shoot Generation: Use generative AI to produce the virtual product photoshoots from textual prompts
- Model/Avatar Selection: Select and customize AI models or avatars for shoots, offering diverse options for representation.
- Scene and Prop Styling: Offers scene gallery, AI-generated scenes, mood presets, lighting, props, storylines and camera suggestions with its AI art director tool.
- Lighting and Animation: Offers controls for shadows and depth via the lighting studio. Catwalk and product spins are other AI-controlled components.
- Product Mode: Upload product images for automated background removal, shadow/reflection adjustment, and multiple-format export.
- Projects and Brand Management: Organize past shoot attempts in the My Projects component; maintain consistency across multiple projects with brand settings.
- Subscription handling: Handle subscriptions payments, tiers, and usage analytics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Brand Yellow (#FFB400) for primary accents, evoking energy and modernity.
- Secondary color: Brand Green (#28A92B) as a secondary accent, adding balance.
- Background color: Deep Navy (#071029) provides a dark, premium backdrop, enhanced with Dark Surface (#0E152E) for elements.
- Headings: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) to ensure a clean, contemporary aesthetic.
- Body: 'Inter' (sans-serif) to maximize readability.
